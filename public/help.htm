Why doesn't it work?

It's like proto-alpha, so things will break, go missing or just plain not work. But they should work, so drop us a line at tim at geothings dot net and it will be looked at.

Why can't I use google maps?

This application is designed for use with the openstreetmap project. There are legal and ethical arguments not to use Google Maps when rectifiying and processing out of copyright maps.

WMS & JOSM

grab the wms link and paste that into the relevant place using the wmsplugin. Have a look on the openstreetmap wiki for more info.
